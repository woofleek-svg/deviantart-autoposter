Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Art Gallery ("we", "our", or "us") operates an automated art sharing service that posts artwork from DeviantArt to Instagram and Tumblr. This privacy policy explains how we handle data in connection with our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Instagram Account Information: Your Instagram Business Account ID and username for the purpose of posting artwork
  • Tumblr Account Information: Your Tumblr blog credentials for posting artwork
  • DeviantArt Content: Public artwork information from DeviantArt (titles, images, URLs) to be shared

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Automatically post artwork from DeviantArt to your Instagram and Tumblr accounts
  • Track which artworks have been posted to prevent duplicates
  • Maintain authentication tokens for API access

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Authentication tokens are encrypted and stored in secure state files.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties. We only interact with Instagram, Tumblr, and DeviantArt APIs to provide the service.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke authorization at any time
