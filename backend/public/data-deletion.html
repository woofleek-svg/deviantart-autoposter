Data Deletion Instructions

What data does Art Gallery store about you?

  • Instagram Business Account ID and username
  • Tumblr blog credentials
  • Authentication tokens for API access
  • History of posted artworks (titles, IDs, timestamps)

Automatic Data Deletion

When you revoke authorization for the Art Gallery app, your authentication tokens are automatically invalidated.

Manual Data Deletion Request

To request complete deletion of your data from our system:

  1. Revoke App Authorization:
    • Go to Instagram Settings → Security → Apps and Websites
    • Remove "Art Gallery" or your app name
    • Go to Tumblr Settings → Apps → Revoke access
  2. Contact Us:

    Email us at your-email@your-domain.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

    Include:

    • Your Instagram username
    • Your Tumblr blog name
    • Any other identifying information
  3. Confirmation:

    We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Important: Deleting your data will permanently stop the automatic posting service. Posts that have already been published to Instagram and Tumblr will remain on those platforms unless you delete them separately.

What Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • All authentication tokens and credentials
  • Your Instagram and Tumblr account information
  • Complete posting history database records
  • Any state files associated with your account

What Remains

The following will NOT be deleted (as they exist on third-party platforms):

  • Posts already published to Instagram
  • Posts already published to Tumblr
  • Original artwork on DeviantArt
